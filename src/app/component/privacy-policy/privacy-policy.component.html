Cookies

When our clients visit our website, the company may assign one or more "cookies" to the client's computers. By accessing our website, all clients/potential clients/visitors agree that we can place cookies on their computers.

Cookies are text files that may be place in the computer's browser to store the user's preference. Cookies by themselves not tell us the user's e-mail address or other personal or sensitive personal information unless the user choose to provide this information to the company.

Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but these can be modify in the browser setting to decline cookies. If the user choose not to allow us to place a cookie on the computer, the user may be restricted from some services and some of the interactive features that are being offered by our website.
